AI Traffic Lights

The AI traffic light was a project designed for the safety of disadvantaged people. This program extends crosswalk times by detecting pedestrians that require extra time such as children, seniors, and those with leg or visual disabilities through a camera attached to a traffic light. Once a camera detects the visually impaired through a white cane or guide dog, AI traffic light alerts through voice. In order to prevent traffic confusion, a distinct algorithm was built.
