ABCEDE

ABCEDE is an automated platform where young global leaders can teach business. It also provides opportunities for mentees to receive feedback from qualified student mentors, interact with participants from all over the world, and learn business in a fun and easy way. This website is built through the utilization of HTML/CSS/JS for the front end and node.js for the server side.
