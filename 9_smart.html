Smartlight

Smartlight was designed to minimize traffic accidents on crosswalks as 71.6% of accidents derived from drivers’ lack of awareness. Also, Smartlight can flexibly adjust traffic lights based on emergency vehicles. The camera is placed several meters before traffic lights. When overspeeding vehicles pass by, the microcomputer predicts its braking distance, thus alerting pedestrians. Also, the microphone detects emergency vehicles’ sirens to minimize their transfer time. Raspberry pi 4GB was utilized for actual modeling.
