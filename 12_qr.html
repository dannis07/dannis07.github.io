QR Phishing Prevention Software

This project implemented crypto technology to strengthen QR code security. Qshing, the act of phishing through QR codes, was on the rise during Covid-19. Thus, I used Python to develop a QR code phishing prevention app that has tolerance to phishing through the usage of HMAC for login and registration that guarantees the integrity of the QR code.
